About This Site

Welcome to my website! My name is James Helms, and I'm passionate about web and software development. This site is dedicated to sharing my knowledge and experiences in the world of coding and design. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, I hope you find valuable resources and inspiration here.

I believe that technology can transform lives, and web development is one of the most dynamic fields that offers endless possibilities. This site serves as a portfolio of my work and experiences.

Feel free to explore the various sections of the site and embark on your journey into the fascinating world of development!

I made this site as a project with Correlation One.

About Me

Hey there! I'm James, a web developer with a passion for creating meaningful and user-friendly digital experiences. My journey in web development started mid-July 2023 with Correlation One. Ever since then, I've been on a quest to learn and grow in this ever-evolving field. As for hobbies, I love the world of Harry Potter! Thats what this website's theme is based around as I am a proud Gryffindor! I love to game and explore different genres of music. I plan on one day either making my own game or being a part of a team that does. I believe that the web has the power to make the world a better place, and my goal is to contribute to that vision. Through this website, I aim to share my knowledge, insights, and creative projects.
